About Higher Secondary Academy

For over two decades, Higher Secondary Academy has been a beacon of academic excellence, nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders. Our institution stands as a testament to quality education, offering separate divisions for boys and girls with world-class facilities.

We believe in holistic development, combining rigorous academics with extracurricular activities, sports, and character building. Our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and student-centric approach create an environment where every student can thrive.

  • State-of-the-art laboratories and libraries
  • Experienced and qualified faculty members
  • Comprehensive sports and wellness programs
  • Individual attention and mentorship

Admission Process

Your journey to excellence begins here - Follow our simple three-step admission process

Join HSA Academy Today

We welcome bright and motivated students who are ready to embark on an exceptional educational journey. Our admission process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our challenging academic environment and contribute positively to our community.

Admissions Open Year-Round
Both Boys & Girls Divisions
Limited Seats Available
01

Primary Evaluation & Test

The first step in our admission process is a comprehensive evaluation test that assesses your academic knowledge, aptitude, and problem-solving skills. This test covers subjects including Mathematics, Science, English, and General Knowledge.

  • Written examination covering core subjects
  • Duration: 2-3 hours
  • Multiple choice and descriptive questions
  • Minimum 60% required to proceed
02

Selection Camp & Interview

Successful candidates from the primary test are invited to our selection camp - an immersive two-day experience where we assess your personality, communication skills, and potential through group activities and personal interviews.

  • Two-day interactive selection camp
  • Group discussions and team activities
  • One-on-one interview with faculty panel
  • Parent-guardian meeting session
03

Final Selection

Based on your performance in the evaluation test and selection camp, our admissions committee makes the final decision. Selected candidates receive an offer letter and can proceed with enrollment formalities.

  • Results announced within 7 working days
  • Offer letter sent via email and post
  • Document verification process
  • Fee payment and enrollment completion
